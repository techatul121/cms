Welcome to MemoryLab. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how MemoryLab handles user information.

1. Information Collection

MemoryLab does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

  • No account registration required
  • No login system
  • No name, email, phone number, or address collected
  • No payment information collected

2. Game Data

MemoryLab may store basic game progress locally on your device such as:

  • Game scores
  • Level progress
  • Settings preferences

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

3. Internet Usage

MemoryLab is designed to work offline. The app does not require an internet connection to function.

4. Third-Party Services

MemoryLab does not integrate with any third-party analytics, tracking, advertising, or marketing services.

5. Children's Privacy

MemoryLab is safe for all age groups. Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no risk of children's data being stored or shared.

6. Data Security

As MemoryLab does not transmit or store personal data online, your privacy and security remain fully protected on your own device.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
